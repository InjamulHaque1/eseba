About Us

Our E-Seba team is a dedicated and diverse group of experts united by a shared mission: to revolutionize healthcare access in Bangladesh. With a range of skills, from development to design and marketing, we've created a user-friendly platform that offers convenient, efficient, and transparent healthcare solutions. Our passion drives us to serve our community's needs relentlessly.

Motivation behind our work

Bangladesh, a nation grappling with poverty and an ailing healthcare system, has provided the impetus for our endeavor. In critical situations, individuals often face the daunting task of navigating a labyrinth of hospitals, leading to prolonged delays. Our solution empowers users with real-time information, enabling them to pinpoint nearby ambulances, ascertain ICU availability, and determine blood availability instantly. Furthermore, E-Seba addresses fundamental healthcare needs, including medical appointments, acquisition of medical accessories, and medication procurement. Our resolve to alleviate the burdens of an inefficient healthcare system has been a driving force behind this initiative.

Impact on our lives

Our initiative, E-Seba, is a catalyst for transformative impact. It champions convenience, offering seamless access to medical care and products, particularly benefiting those with mobility constraints and remote residents. Through online appointments and prescriptions, we deliver swift, efficient healthcare, reducing waiting times. In emergencies, we provide immediate access to critical resources like ambulances, blood donations, and ICUs. Our digital record management streamlines patient care while minimizing paperwork. Geographical barriers dissolve, ensuring equitable healthcare access for underserved areas. We prioritize health education, empowering individuals with vital information. Transparency in transactions and treatment plans builds trust, elevating the healthcare experience for all..
